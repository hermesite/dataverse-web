A VR Homage to David Bowie and his influence

  • Visit source Google Sheet

This VR work is a homage to the great David Robert Jones (8 January 1947 - 10 January 2016), AKA David Bowie. It's a short VR tour around his life, influence and huge artistic talent. You'll virtually visit places, see data visualizations and enjoy his audiovisual legacy.
